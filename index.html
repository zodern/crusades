Crusades

Began When

Muslims were entering Byzantine territory. Byzantine emperor Alexius asked Pope Urban II for help against the muslims.

Fun Fact

Everyone who participated in the crusades were forgiven of all their sins.

First Crusade
1095 - 1099

Pope Urban II started the first crusade at the Council of Clermont in 1095.

Goal was to free Palestin and Jerusalem from the Muslims.

They captured Jerusalem in 1099. The crusaders massacred many people when entering, even though they said they wouldn't.

Created 4 crusader states to protect the land they gained. The states were Jerusalem, Edessa, Antioch, and Tripoli.

The crusader state Edessa was captured in 1144 by the Turks.

Second Crusade
1147 - 1149

Because Edessa was lost in 1144, they decided to send a second crusade.

Ended when the French and German armies failed to take Damascus

Third Crusade
1189 - 1192

Led by Richard 1 from England, Philip II from France, and Federeick 1 from the Holy Roman Emperor.

In September 1191 Richard I defeated Saladin's army in the battle of Arsuf. It was the only real battle in the third crusade

Ended when Richard I and the Muslim leader Saladin compromised and allowed Christians access to the holy places.
